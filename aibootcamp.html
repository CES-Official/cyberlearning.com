What is the Microsoft Learn for Educators (MSLE) AI Bootcamps?

The Microsoft Learn for Educators (MSLE) AI Bootcamps provide academic institutions with the knowledge and skills to include AI-related education in their courses and programs of study.

The bootcamps train educators and faculty on how to best deliver courses to enhance or develop an AI training program to empower students with the skills needed to succeed in an AI-driven job market.

Who can benefit from the MSLE AI Bootcamps?

MSLE AI Bootcamps are aimed at those faculty and educators looking to build AI skills and incorporate AI into their classes or curriculum. Each bootcamp is tailored for specific AI concepts and skills.

What does an MSLE AI Bootcamp look like?

  • Learn how to add AI skills training to your current courses
  • Gain skills in Azure AI tools and technologies
  • Discover different approaches to program integration
  • Receive direction on Microsoft Credentials for AI skills
  • Find out how to get the most out of Microsoft Learn for Educators (MSLE) and its many teaching resources

Who delivers an MSLE AI Bootcamp?

The MSLE AI Bootcamps are a fee-based service offered by Microsoft Training Services Partners (TSP). TSPs provide a variety of training solutions, including blended learning, in-person, and online options, tailored to your learning needs. TSPs around the world have met program requirements and have been trained to deliver the MSLE AI Bootcamps and more.

The MSLE AI Bootcamp sessions are adaptable based on the needs of the academic institution. Tailored training includes in-person, virtual, and hybrid learning. Depending on the TSP, bootcamp sessions can be offered in various time zones and languages.
